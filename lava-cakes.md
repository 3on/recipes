# Lava cakes
_AKA panty droppers_

## Ingredients

_This is for a small batch, 10 or less, I usually do x2 to x4 and froze the dough_
- 100g of 70% chocolate
- 120g of sugar
- 90g of butter
- 3 eggs
- 40g flour
- a fuckload of amaretto

## Steps

1. Melt chocolate and butter
2. Mix eggs and sugar
3. Mix 1 and 2
4. Add the flour
